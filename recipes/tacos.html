Tacos

Description

Tacos are a beloved Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla filled with a variety of ingredients. This recipe features seasoned ground beef or chicken, topped with fresh vegetables, cheese, and a tangy salsa. Tacos are incredibly versatile and can be customized with your favorite toppings and fillings, making them a perfect choice for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 500g ground beef or chicken
  • 1 packet taco seasoning mix
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 8 taco shells or tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded lettuce
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup salsa
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish, optional)
  • Lime wedges (for serving, optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the ground beef or chicken and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon as it cooks.
  2. Drain any excess fat from the skillet. Stir in the taco seasoning mix and a splash of water (as directed on the seasoning packet). Simmer for 5 minutes, allowing the flavors to combine.
  3. While the meat is cooking, prepare the taco shells or tortillas according to package instructions.
  4. To assemble the tacos, spoon a portion of the seasoned meat into each taco shell or tortilla.
  5. Top with shredded lettuce, diced tomatoes, chopped onion, and shredded cheddar cheese.
  6. Finish with a dollop of sour cream and a spoonful of salsa. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with lime wedges, if desired.
